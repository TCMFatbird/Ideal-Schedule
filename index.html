Click on a cell to add a class or edit an existing one. Hold and drag it to move it across the table.

Click on the "Save" button to save your schedule as a JSON file. You can also click on the "Load" button to load a previously saved schedule.

Click on the "Clear" button to clear your schedule.

Click on the "Export as PNG" button to export your schedule as a PNG image.
